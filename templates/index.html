About the Project

Understanding the challenges of manual P&ID processing and how our AI solution addresses them

The Challenge of P&ID Diagrams

Piping and Instrumentation Diagrams (P&IDs) are complex engineering schematics that represent the functional relationship between piping, instrumentation, and system components in industrial processes.

Engineers and operators face significant challenges when working with these diagrams:

  • Time-consuming manual data extraction
  • Error-prone information transfer process
  • Difficulty in keeping documentation synchronized
  • Inconsistencies between physical systems and documentation

Our AI Solution

Our project leverages advanced AI technologies to automatically extract, analyze, and digitize information from P&ID diagrams, enabling seamless integration with digital systems and significant time savings.

How It Works
